Effective date: July 9, 2026

Framatic is a simple camera framing app by Duet LLC. This policy explains what the app does with your information.

## What Framatic Collects

Framatic does not create user accounts and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, or other personal details.

Framatic does not use ads, in-app analytics, crash-reporting SDKs, or third-party tracking.

## Camera and Photos

Framatic uses your camera only to show the live viewfinder and capture photos when you tap the capture button.

Photo processing happens on your device. Framatic does not upload your photos to our servers.

When you save a photo, Framatic writes the finished image to your device gallery. Saved photos stay on your device unless you delete them.

Framatic may create temporary image files while preparing the preview. These files are used only for photo processing and are cleaned up by the app.

## App Settings

Framatic stores your frame settings and quick-access frame order on your device. These settings are used to make the app work the way you configured it.

## Google Play

If you install Framatic through Google Play or join a Google Play test, Google may provide Duet LLC with Play Console information such as install statistics, crash and performance information, ratings, reviews, and private tester feedback.

This information comes from Google Play, not from tracking code added inside Framatic. Google handles this information under Google's own privacy policy and your Google account settings.

## Permissions

Framatic asks for camera permission so it can show the viewfinder and take photos.

Framatic may ask for gallery or storage access so it can save finished photos to your device gallery. On older Android versions, this may use Android's legacy storage permission.

You can deny permissions, but camera capture or photo saving may not work without them.

## Sharing and Selling Data

Framatic does not collect or sell your data.

## Your Choices

You can change app permissions in your device settings.

You can delete saved Framatic photos from your device gallery.

You can delete the app to remove app data stored on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if Framatic changes how it handles data. If the policy changes, we will update the effective date.
